# CRONOS
## Compact Rocket Outlining and Numerical Optimization Simulator
Simplified rocket design and flight simulator for amateur liquid rocketry.

## Features
- 3-DoF trajectory module incorporating supersonic drag effects (WIP)
- Internal component design and organization module (WIP)
- Low-fidelity propulsion module with hooks to interface with more advanced propulsion models (WIP)
- Multidisciplinary design optimization tools (WIP)

## Name
In ancient Greek mythology, Cronos (also spelled Cronus or Kronos) was the leader of the Titans, the youngest son of the earth Gaia and sky Uranus, who personified the harvest and passage of time.
